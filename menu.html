We guarantee our customers the freshness of their order by ensuring that our ingredients are prepared every morning by skillful, exceptional and experienced personnel dedicated to providing the customer with a unique experience of Chinese food and drinks. Our annual sushi-eating competition has become popular and well known to serve the best fish dishes in the region and our customers can attest to that fact. It is not just the food we are focused on but the overall customer experience, which is like the icing on the cake. .

Masori restaurant has a unique historical, cultural, and architectural attributes which add to its aesthetic extravagance with some Chinese royalty displayed all around. At Masori, we believe not only in the satisfaction of customers but also of our staff since it is the staff who make sure that our customers get the best experience possible. A complimentary drink is a tradition that we have always held on to since the restaurant was established and for us, it is a way of sharing with our customers the unique drinks that are made from a range of natural fruits. Most people have discovered one or two drinks or cocktails from our restaurant that have instantly become their favorite and who know maybe more customer will be lucky to experience the same discovery. We take pride in being part of such great experiences. We have managed to open one other venture that solely focuses on spicy food and spices in general. We take pride and find satisfaction in what we do on a daily basis since it comes from the heart with love.
